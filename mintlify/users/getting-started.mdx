# Getting Started with Delight

Welcome to Delight! This guide will help you get started with your AI companion and begin your journey toward achieving your goals.

## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    1. Visit [delight.com](https://delight.com)
    2. Click "Sign Up"
    3. Choose your preferred sign-in method:
       - Email and password
       - Google
       - GitHub
       - Magic link (passwordless)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    Answer a few questions to help us understand:
    - What goals you want to achieve
    - Your preferred communication style
    - When you'd like to receive check-ins
    - Your availability and schedule
  </Step>

  <Step title="Meet Your Companion">
    Get introduced to Eliza, your primary AI companion who will:
    - Help you break down your goals
    - Generate daily missions
    - Provide emotional support
    - Celebrate your progress
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your First Goal">
    Work with Eliza to define your first goal:
    - What do you want to achieve?
    - Why is this important to you?
    - What's challenging about it?
    - When would you like to achieve it?
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Your First Mission">
    Receive your first daily mission and:
    - Complete the task
    - Upload evidence (optional)
    - Reflect on your experience
    - Get feedback from Eliza
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

### Daily Missions

Every day, Delight generates personalized missions based on:
- Your goals and priorities
- Your past performance
- Your current emotional state
- Time you have available

<Info>
**Tip:** Complete missions consistently to build momentum and unlock narrative events!
</Info>

### AI Companions

Chat with AI companions who:
- **Remember** your conversations and context
- **Adapt** their communication style to yours
- **Respond** empathetically to your emotions
- **Support** you without judgment

### Progress Tracking

Monitor your journey with:
- **Streak tracking** - See how many days you've stayed consistent
- **DCI metrics** - Daily Commitment Index shows your dedication
- **Achievement gallery** - Celebrate milestones
- **Highlight reels** - Review your best moments

### Narrative World

Experience your progress through story:
- Real-world achievements unlock narrative events
- Character relationships evolve based on your actions
- Discover hidden quests through exploration
- Shape the story with your choices

## Tips for Success

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start Small">
    Don't try to change everything at once. Focus on one main goal and build from there.

    **Example:**
    - ❌ "Get fit, learn Spanish, start a business, read 50 books"
    - ✅ "Exercise for 20 minutes, 3x per week"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Be Honest with Eliza">
    Your AI companion can only help if you're honest about:
    - When you're struggling
    - What's actually challenging
    - Your real availability
    - Your true feelings

    There's no judgment - Eliza is here to support you!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Build Streaks Gradually">
    You don't need a perfect streak to make progress:
    - 3-4 days per week is better than nothing
    - Consistency matters more than perfection
    - Recovery from breaks is part of the journey
    - Focus on progress, not perfection
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use Evidence Uploads">
    Uploading evidence makes your achievements tangible:
    - Take photos of your progress
    - Share screenshots of milestones
    - Record videos of accomplishments
    - Build a visual gallery of growth

    These become powerful reminders on difficult days!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Engage with the Narrative">
    The story enhances your experience:
    - Read narrative updates after missions
    - Explore character interactions
    - Discover hidden quests
    - Make choices that matter to you

    The story adapts to YOUR journey!
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customization

### Communication Preferences

Adjust how Delight communicates:
- **Tone:** Encouraging, direct, gentle, playful
- **Frequency:** How often you receive check-ins
- **Channels:** In-app, email, push notifications
- **Timing:** When notifications are sent

### Availability Settings

Set your schedule:
- **Active hours:** When you're available for missions
- **Focus time:** When you don't want interruptions
- **Weekend settings:** Different schedule for weekends
- **Time zones:** Automatic adjustment for travel

### Privacy Controls

Manage your data:
- **Conversation history:** Choose what to keep/delete
- **Evidence visibility:** Public, friends-only, or private
- **Data export:** Download your data anytime
- **Account deletion:** Full data removal option

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does it cost?">
    Delight offers:
    - **Free tier:** Basic features and limited missions
    - **Premium:** $1/day for full access and unlimited missions
    - **Pay-as-you-go:** Premium features like advanced narrative

    Start with the free tier to see if Delight is right for you!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change my goal?">
    You can update goals anytime:
    1. Go to Goals section
    2. Select the goal to modify
    3. Chat with Eliza about the change
    4. Confirm the new direction

    Your missions will adapt to the updated goal.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I miss a day?">
    Missing a day is okay!
    - Your streak resets, but progress doesn't
    - Eliza will check in compassionately
    - Get back on track without guilt
    - Focus on the next mission

    **Remember:** Recovery is part of growth!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple goals?">
    Yes! You can work on:
    - 1 primary goal (main focus)
    - 2-3 secondary goals (supporting)
    - Unlimited tracked goals (minimal missions)

    Start with one and add more as you build momentum.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data private?">
    Absolutely:
    - Conversations are encrypted
    - Data is never sold or shared
    - You control visibility settings
    - Export or delete anytime

    See our [Privacy Policy](#) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
